Teaching

As much as I enjoy scientific research, I also enjoy and prioritize teaching both in and out of the classroom. My teaching philosophy is to prioritize building curiosity, developing skills, and fostering scientific thinking. I focus on big-picture concepts, transferrable skills, and thinking through examples, rather than on recalling any given piece of information, viewing much of the factual content as a means to these ends and as an opportunity to engage student interest. I have taught human evolution as instructor of record, as well as multiple classes as a teaching assistance (see CV for details). I am currently an NIH IRACDA postdoctoral fellow with the Emory FIRST program and will teaching at one of our partner institutions (Spelman College, Morehouse College, or Clark Atlanta University).
